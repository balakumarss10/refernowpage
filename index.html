About the Programme

At 21K School, Asia’s Leading Online school, we are all about building our community and we are so pleased that the word is out. More than 80% of our new families knew someone at 21K School before they registered with us, so THANK YOU for sharing our school with your friends

In recognition of this we are Introducing the 21K School Referral Programme. A one-of-a-kind opportunity for you, our current families to engage more deeply with some of the best resources that 21K School has to offer.

This referral programme is not only a special way of thanking you as a 21K School parent for your trust and partnership with us, but also to encourage you to leverage this opportunity and become more involved within the school community.

Our Promise to you & your Friend

When you recommend a friend to 21K School, please tell them we’ll be in touch. We will make a single connection with them to talk about our school, training and career opportunities. If they’re interested, we’ll continue the conversation. If they aren’t, we promise to let it end there because we value your trust and respect your friendship.

Terms & Conditions

  • The Student Referral Program is open to all parents, or legal guardians enrolled in 21K School, but it does not apply to new sibling students.
  • Employees and associates of 21K School, even when their students study in the 21K School, cannot participate in the Student Referral Program directly or through affiliates.
  • A tuition fee waiver will be given for referring new families/students to 21K School (does not include returning families).
  • To qualify for the incentive, referred students cannot have a sibling currently or previously enrolled in 21K School.
  • All referrals must be acknowledged by the new family using the unique referral link only during admission and not after admission. To receive the tuition fee waiver, the referring family (current 21K School Parents/Legal Guardians) must ensure that the new students sign up using the unique referral link by entering their mobile number and email id.
  • The tuition fee waiver will be applied to your last Instalment of the academic year. All benefits shall lapse if the referred student discontinues 21K School before the completion of the academic year.
  • The Tuition fee waiver is applied only to the Annual School Tuition Fees. It does not apply to Learning Kits, Coding Kits, Examination Fees, Centre Charges, Language Fees, SEN fees, Day Boarding Fees and similar.
  • The tuition fee waiver will be applied to the fees as applicable to the unique referral's child. The benefits are passed only to the younger child if a parent has more than one child. It cannot be transferred, edited or split. The same applies to the new students enrolling.
  • Referral rebates cannot be combined in conjunction with any other promotions/discounts or privileges obtained during admission.
  • The Student Referral Program is subject to review or suspension at any time. Interpretation of program parameters will be at the discretion of the 21K School Management.
  • There is no limit to the number of student referrals. However, this is optional and voluntary. Students can enrol at 21K School without any referrals from existing parents.
